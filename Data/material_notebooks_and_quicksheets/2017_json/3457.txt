{"id": 3457, "text": "LOS 58.g\nIn a simple interest-rate swap, one party pays a floating rate and the other pays a fixed\nrate on a notional principal amount. The first payment is known at initiation and\nthe rest of the payments are unknown. The unknown payments are equivalent to the\npayments on off-market FRAs. To replicate a swap with a value of zero at initiation, the\nsum of the present values of these FRAs must equal zero.\n"}
