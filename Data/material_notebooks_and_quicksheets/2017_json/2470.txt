{"id": 2470, "text": "Clearinghouses and Custodians\nClearinghouses act as intermediaries between buyers and sellers in financial markets and\nprovide:\n\u2022 Escrow services (transferring cash and assets to the respective parties).\nGuarantees of contract completion.\nAssurance that margin traders have adequate capital.\nLimits on the aggregate net order quantity (buy orders minus sell orders) of\nmembers.\nThrough these activities, clearinghouses limit counterparty risk, the risk that the other\nparty to a transaction will not fulfill its obligation. In some markets, the clearinghouse\nensures only the trades of its member brokers and dealers, who, in turn, ensure the\ntrades of their retail customers.\n"}
