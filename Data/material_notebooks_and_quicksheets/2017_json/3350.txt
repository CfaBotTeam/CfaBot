{"id": 3350, "text": "OPTIONS\nAn option contract gives its owner the right, but not the obligation, to either buy or sell\nan underlying asset at a given price (the exercise price or strike price). While an option\nbuyer can choose whether to exercise an option, the seller is obligated to perform if the\nbuyer exercises the option.\n\u2022 The owner of a call option has the right to purchase the underlying asset at a specific\nprice for a specified time period.\nThe owner of a put option has the right to sell the underlying asset at a specific price\nfor a specified time period.\n"}
