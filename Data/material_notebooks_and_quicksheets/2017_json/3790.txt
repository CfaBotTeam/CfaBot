{"id": 3790, "text": "A forward rate is a borrowing/lending rate for a loan to be made at some future date.\nThe notation used must identify both the length of the lending/borrowing period and\nwhen in the future the money will be loaned/borrowed. Thus, lyly is the rate for a\n1-year loan one year from now; 2yly is the rate for a 1-year loan to be made two years\nfrom now; 3y2y is the 2-year forward rate three years from now; and so on.\n"}
