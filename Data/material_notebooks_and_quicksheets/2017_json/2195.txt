{"id": 2195, "text": "Subjective and Market-Based Estimates of Risk\nTwo methods of risk assessment that are used to supplement measures such as Var\nand CVaR are stress testing and scenario analysis. Stress testing examines the effects\nof a specific (usually extreme) change in a key variable such as an interest rate or\nexchange rate. Scenario analysis refers to a similar what-if analysis of expected loss but\nincorporates changes in multiple inputs. A given scenario might combine an interest rate\nchange with a significant change in oil prices or exchange rates.\n"}
