{"id": 1777, "text": "Working capital (sometimes called net working capital) is current assets minus\ncurrent liabilities. The working capital turnover ratio gives us information about\nthe utilization of working capital in terms of dollars of sales per dollar of working\ncapital. Some firms may have very low working capital if outstanding payables\nequal or exceed inventory and receivables. In this case the working capital turnover\nratio will be very large, may vary significantly from period to period, and is less\ninformative about changes in the firm's operating efficiency.\n"}
